Food Exchanges


A food exchange refers to each food items on a particular list and the portion listed may be substituted with any other food item
on the same list. In other words, it can be a substitute, choice or serving (FNRI, 2008).

Below is the list of food exchanges that used to classify and group the common goods in terms of equivalent amounts of
Carbohydrates, Protein, Fats, and Calories.
