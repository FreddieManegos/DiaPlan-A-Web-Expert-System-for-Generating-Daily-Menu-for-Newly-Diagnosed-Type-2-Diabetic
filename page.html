About Us


DiaPlan is an expert system for generating meal plan for newly diagnosed Type-2 Diabetes. It brings you information about diabetes specially in monitoring your meal plan to take control of your blood sugar level. It also includes the Glycemic Test to let you know wether your blood sugar is at normal state or not. DiaPlan offers reliable, meal plan which is locally available and an up-to-date information about diabetes, anytime, anywhere, for free.


This system is created by Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Students for the fulfillment of Thesis Requirement. Guided and tested by registered Dietitians of hospitals in Iligan City.
